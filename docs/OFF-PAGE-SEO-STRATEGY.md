### Broken Link Building
1. Use Ahrefs/SEMrush to find broken links on tech blogs
2. Create content that matches the broken link topic
3. Reach out to site owner with replacement suggestion

## Phase 4: Authority Building (Month 2-3)

### HARO (Help A Reporter Out)
- Sign up at helpareporter.com
- Respond to tech/digital lifestyle queries
- Get featured in online publications

### Infographic Creation
- Create shareable infographics about:
  - "AI Tools Comparison 2026"
  - "Streaming Services Indonesia"
  - "Laptop Buying Guide"
- Submit to infographic directories
- Offer to tech blogs for republishing

### Expert Roundups
- Participate in expert roundup posts
- Host your own roundup, invite influencers
